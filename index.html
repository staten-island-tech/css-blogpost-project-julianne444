✧ Crystals to Keep in Your Pocket ✧

Whether you're heading to school or a job interview, having these crystals with you for luck and abundance is always a great idea.

.

Tiger's Eye

Tiger's Eye is a golden-brown stone that taps into one's self-confidence. It is also protective, not just in the physical but in the mental and spiritual as well. Expect to step out of your comfort zone more with this stone in hand.

Rhodonite

Rhodonite, similar to another pink stone, Rose Quartz, promotes one's self-love and compassion. Emotional scars or damage can be cleared away with the healing properties of this crystal.

Aventurine

Aventurine, is said to be the stone of luck and prosperity. Carrying this with you can help you manifest new opportunities or just give you that boost of fortune when finding a parking spot for example.
